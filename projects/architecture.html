Architecture

Since 2012, I have been working as an architect with a particular interest in wood design. Throughout my career, I have been drawn to the unique characteristics and aesthetic qualities of wood, and have developed a deep understanding of the material's potential for use in architecture and design.

In addition to my work as an architect and my interest in wood design, I have also gained extensive experience in industrial processes through my work in the food and beverage industry. During my time working in the food and beverage industry, I gained a deep understanding of how kitchens are designed to optimize functionality, efficiency, and safety. I learned about the importance of effective spatial planning, the selection of appropriate equipment, and the implementation of effective workflows to maximize productivity and minimize waste.
