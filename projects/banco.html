Banking Blog

I worked on designing components for a CRM system for a banking blog, my primary objective was to create an intuitive and user-friendly interface that would allow users to efficiently manage their blog's content

To achieve this, I conducted extensive user research that involved user interviews, surveys, and usability tests to gain insights from the target audience. Based on this research, I developed user personas and user journeys to guide the design process. I began the design process by creating wireframes and low-fidelity prototypes, allowing me to quickly iterate and test various design concepts, and make changes based on user feedback.

Throughout the design process, I worked closely with the bank's design team to ensure that the designs aligned with the bank's brand guidelines and design standards. Finally, I worked with the development team to ensure that the designs were implemented accurately and efficiently. Throughout the development process, I conducted quality assurance testing to ensure that the final product met the user's needs and was easy to use.
