Shipping Portal

Cargo Expreso Shipping Portal is a web app designed to streamline the logistics and shipping processes of the Cargo Expreso company. The app provides an intuitive platform for managing shipments, tracking packages, and communicating with customers.

Users can:

  • Create Shipments: Quickly and easily create new shipments with customizable options for pickup and delivery times, packaging details, and more.
  • Track Packages: Monitor the status and location of packages in real-time with detailed tracking information and alerts.
  • Manage Deliveries: Schedule and manage delivery times and locations with customizable options for pickup and delivery windows.
  • Communicate with Customers: Connect with customers directly through the app with built-in messaging tools and real-time status updates.

Cargo Expreso is the ultimate tool in Guatemala for businesses looking to streamline their logistics and shipping processes. Whether you're shipping products across town or across the country, Cargo Expreso can help simplify your operations and provide a better experience for your customers. With a range of customizable options and real-time tracking and communication tools, Cargo Expreso is the go-to platform for businesses looking to optimize their logistics and shipping processes.

As the design lead for Cargo Expreso since 2020, I have been responsible for creating and implementing a new user interface for the web app. Working closely with the development team, we chose to use Oracle Visual Builder as our development platform to create a modern and intuitive user experience for our customers.

Our new design focuses on simplifying the shipping and logistics process for our customers, making it easier for them to create shipments, track packages, and manage their deliveries. We incorporated user feedback and industry best practices to create a streamlined workflow that is both efficient and user-friendly.

Since the initial release of the new user interface for Cargo Expreso on Oracle Visual Builder, the project has continued to evolve. We have been working closely with our customers to gather feedback and make improvements to the platform, resulting in a more user-friendly and efficient experience.
